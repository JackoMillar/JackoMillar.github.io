About me!

Jack Millar is a dedicated software engineering student at Napier University in Edinburgh, where he's been immersed in his studies since 2022. His journey into the world of software and game development has been driven by a passion for technology and innovation. Jack has developed a strong foundation in various programming languages, including HTML, Java, C, C++, C#, and Haskell. His coursework and projects at university have given him hands-on experience with tools like Android Studio and .NET frameworks, preparing him for a future in the tech industry.

Since 2020, Jack has also been a Senior Team Member at Gregg’s plc. In this role, he consistently delivers exceptional customer service, ensuring that every patron has a positive experience. Jack's responsibilities include managing team members, efficiently delegating tasks, handling cash transactions, and maintaining the store’s cleanliness and organization. His ability to balance these tasks demonstrates his strong organizational and teamwork skills, which are essential in any professional setting.

Outside of his academic and work commitments, Jack actively participates in the game modding community. He enjoys enhancing gameplay and creating new content for existing games, which has further sharpened his programming skills. Jack is proficient in using the Unity Engine and has a solid understanding of online gaming architecture, including server and client-side development, having spent time in and outside of class working towards projects. Jack’s problem-solving abilities, adaptability, and passion for innovation make him a valuable asset in the field of software engineering.
