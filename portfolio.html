Lua

Mod Developer

Repository

Outside of school, Jack actively engaged in designing and developing various projects for the indie game Warzone.com (not to be confused with COD: Warzone). Over two years, he released 15 mods to the public, each contributing to his proficiency in Lua and laying a strong foundation for future endeavors.

In addition to his role as a mod developer, Jack serves as the Main Moderator for the game's Modding Discord Server. Well-regarded within the community, he is frequently available to support and mentor newcomers in the modding space.
