Greggs Senior Team Member

(2020 - present)

Working as a senior team member at Greggs involves leading and supporting a team in delivering excellent customer service, ensuring high standards of food quality and hygiene, managing inventory, and assisting with store operations. Responsibilities include training new staff, managing end-of-day financial procedures, handling customer queries and contributing to sales targets. The role requires strong communication skills, leadership abilities, and a good understanding of the company's products and values.

Jack Millar currently has four years of experience in the food service industry, during of which has involved taking charge of a store in a fast-paced drive-thru setting. Jack's responsibilities have included contributing to the training of multiple new staff members, ensuring they are well-equipped to deliver exceptional service. Jack is well-liked and well-known among his colleagues and customers for his dedication and positive attitude.

In addition to Jack's regular duties, he is known to be available for extra shifts to lighten other's workloads as well as have traveled to assist in the running of other local stores. This flexibility and willingness to support the broader team demonstrates Jack's commitment to the company's success and his passion for the role.

In 2022, Jack was recognized as a role model for others as he achieved the Top Sales Performer at their store as well as accounted for the highest number of transactions in the same period.
